6. Story log is only 7 entries
storyLog is sliced to 7 lines. It's used for mission events, license level-ups, contract completions — but 7 entries vanish fast in active play. Bumping to 20 with maybe a scrollable history view in the HUD would let players track what happened in the last 10 minutes.

7. Anomaly field variety
The type system supports push | pull | drag | ion | slipstream but most anomalies in universe.ts use pull. A drag anomaly (velocity cap inside the zone) or a slipstream (speed bonus in one direction) would create differentiated tactical situations.

8. More transport missions
There are ~8 static transport missions. These are premium-feel content — named cargo, specific routes, time bonuses. Adding 4-5 more spanning the frontier regions (Ironbound and Veilborn routes) would fill the gap between early game and procgen contracts feeling generic.

My recommendation for where to start: Regional events + site hotspots first (pure data, zero risk, immediate world-feel payoff), then a fourth boss mission to cap the story chain, then the standing unlock rewards. Those three together are probably 80% of the experience delta for 30-40% of the work.
